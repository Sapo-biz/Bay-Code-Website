Our Mission

Bay Code is a student-founded and student-led organization based in the Bay Area that empowers the next generation of coders through inclusive, logic-focused programming events. Our mission is to make coding accessible, collaborative, and genuinely fun, especially for students with little to no prior experience.

Whether you're brand new to programming or an experienced developer looking to test your skills, our events are built to meet you where you areâ€”and take you further.
